Introduction What is Citeplasm™?

Citeplasm aims to be a complete research stack for students, academic professionals, and researchers. Citeplasm is both a revolutionary way of looking at information and an integrated toolset for all four steps of the process: discovery, development, documentation, and distribution.

Discovery: the search for information. Citeplasm saves time by providing semantically-linked articles and data sets from governments and academic organizations around the world. All in one place, all integrated, all reliable.

Development: generating hypotheses, refining ideas. Citeplasm...

Documentation: incorporating ideas into a paper. Citeplasm...

Distribution: painless formatting and transmission. Citeplasm...

Citeplasm plans to revolutionize the way we do research by providing unprecedented visibility into data from government, industry, and academia throughout the world, integrated for the first time in a meaningful and complete way. Here's how Citeplasm will positively impact each target demographic:

Students

It will provide a thoroughly modern and exciting one-stop solution for each stage of the research process: discovery of existing knowledge, development or original ideas, documentation of those ideas, and finally distribution of a completed work, which itself becomes part of the existing knowledge base. Each step build on all the others, exposing rich relationships into which existing tools provide no visibility.

The Bottom Line: students can research, write, and distribute a paper more accurately and more efficiently than ever, all from one easy-to-use tool.
