Development

Documentation

Distribution

Finally, simply specify a format like PDF, a style like MLA, and a destination and Citeplasm will do the rest - including citations. Citeplasm FTW!

Simplifying Research Through Innovation

Citeplasm collects reliable info from government, industry, and academia and employs artificial intelligence and machine learning to actually understand that info so it can integrate it together a meaningful way.

It knows that "Thomas Jefferson" mentioned in a textbook is a person, who is the same as the U.S. President who signed the Louisiana Purchase, and the same as the one who penned the Declaration of Independence.

Citeplasm connects tens of millions of articles, videos, and documents, so when you want info specifically about your topic, you can easily slice and dice and query it any way you need it.

Citeplasm is like an extension of your mind.
