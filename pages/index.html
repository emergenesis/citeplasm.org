About Emergenesis Citeplasm™

From open data sets to authoring and publishing tools, it has everything needed for serious research.

Discovery

Citeplasm is built on top of Knowledge Graph™ - a massive web of semantically-linked open data from governmental and academic organizations around the world.

Development

With the ability to annotate the graph and elucidate concepts, Citeplasm provides an intuitive way to organize thoughts, always connected to where they originated.

Documentation

Building on top of your thoughts, Citeplasm provides a web-based format- and style-agnostic document editor so you can write from anywhere, focusing only on what matters - the content.

Distribution

Finally, Citeplasm knows how to format and stylize a document. Simply specify a format like PDF and a style like MLA and Citeplasm does the rest. Nothing could be simpler.

Simplifying Research Through Innovation

While humans think in webs of directed relationships, our tools do not. To bring research more in line with the way we process information requires cutting-edge technology and a little ingenuity, but the result is awesome: an easy to use research stack that behaves like an extension of your mind.

To understand the true value of these webs, try out our interactive demonstration of Scholarship Graph, or read our design docs.
