About Emergenesis Citeplasm™

From open data sets to authoring and publishing tools, it has everything needed for serious research.

Discovery

Citeplasm has millions of reliable data from governments and universities around the world, so it's easy to find stuff about your topic.

Development

You can organise the info any way that makes sense to you, and then use that info to generate original ideas.

Documentation

Now you can compose your thoughts into a document, not having to worry at all about line spacing, margins, and junk.

Distribution

Finally, simply specify a format like PDF, a style like MLA, and a destination and Citeplasm will do the rest. Citeplasm FTW!

Simplifying Research Through Innovation

While humans think in webs of directed relationships; our tools do not. To bring research more in line with the way we process information requires cutting-edge technology and a little ingenuity, but the result is awesome: an easy to use research stack that behaves like an extension of your mind.

To understand the true value of these webs, try out our interactive demonstration of Scholarship Graph, or read our design docs.
